Delete Your Spirit Library Account

You can request deletion of your Spirit Library account and associated data at any time. Follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Spirit Library app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom right).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at stevensamori@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email address, user ID)
  • Your saved cocktail lists and collections
  • Your custom cocktails
  • Your My Bar ingredient preferences
  • Your username and profile information

Data Retention

All personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion with no retention period. Anonymized, non-identifiable usage analytics data (such as aggregate app interaction counts) may be retained for up to 90 days for statistical purposes only.

Partial Data Deletion

If you would like to delete specific data without deleting your account (such as your saved lists or My Bar ingredients), you can do so directly within the app at any time.
