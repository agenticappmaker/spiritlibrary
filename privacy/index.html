Privacy Policy

Spirit Library — Last updated: April 1, 2026

Spirit Library ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Spirit Library mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those services.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics data to understand how the app is used — for example, which screens are visited most. This data cannot identify you personally unless you grant tracking permission when prompted.

User Content: Cocktail lists, saved recipes, and custom cocktails you create are stored in your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the App
  • To sync your saved data across devices
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on servers in the United States.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage
  • Google Sign-In — optional login method
  • PostHog — anonymous analytics

Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

Children

Spirit Library is intended for users aged 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17.
