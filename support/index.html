Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Email us at stevensamori@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we'll remove your data within 30 days.

My app is crashing — what should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall Spirit Library from the App Store, then email us with details.

How do I save a cocktail?
Tap any cocktail card to open it, then tap the bookmark icon to save it to your collection.

Can I create my own recipes?
Yes — go to the Saved tab and tap "Create My Own Cocktail" to build and save a custom recipe.

How does My Bar work?
Go to the My Bar tab and type in the ingredients you have at home. Spirit Library will show every cocktail you can make with what you have.
